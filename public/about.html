About OverEdge

We use data and probabilistic modeling to surface football edges—simple to read, fast to act on.

Free Picks

Up to two daily European fixtures that meet our confidence/odds filters.

Pro Board

Full slate for our allowed leagues ranked by confidence with quick “Top Bets”.

Hero Bet

One featured play from tier-1 leagues with the strongest calibrated edge.

How it Works (under the hood)

  • Inputs: Team statistics from API-Football (via /teams/statistics) blended across current & prior seasons.
  • Model: A Poisson goals framework estimates λHome, λAway, expected goals, P(Over 2.5), P(BTTS), and 1X2 probabilities.
  • Scoring: Heuristics score Over/Under 2.5 and Under 2.5 using pace & historical O2.5%; optional odds give small boosts.
  • Calibration: Raw confidences are mapped through calibration.json so displayed % better reflect reality.
  • Cards/Corners: Simple pace-based chips for totals—shown as guidance, not official picks.

What shows on each page

  • Free Picks: 0–2 selections from European domestic leagues (youth/women/reserve excluded, and excluding Pro’s allow-list). If the primary pool is empty, we may include UEFA or domestic cups.
  • Pro Board: All qualifying fixtures from our allow-list. Each row shows match, league, kickoff, and Top Bets (OU 2.5, BTTS, 1X2, Cards O/U, Corners O/U) with calibrated confidence.
  • Hero Bet: Best single calibrated edge among tier-1 leagues, with a short reasoning (ExpG and λs).

Leagues & Scope

We focus on Europe’s domestic leagues (youth/women/reserve filtered), plus UEFA competitions where relevant. Pro’s exact allow-list and Hero’s tier-1 set are configurable and may evolve across seasons.

Data, Limits & Timing

  • Timings: Picks update by date (UTC). Odds are optional and may be missing for lower-tier fixtures.
  • Data limits: Early-season fixtures blend prior-season stats until a minimum sample accrues.
  • Outages: If API keys or providers rate-limit, you might briefly see fewer picks.

Responsible Betting

OverEdge provides informational picks only—not financial advice. Always bet responsibly, never risk more than you can afford to lose, and follow your local laws (18+ or as required).

Privacy & Security

  • Auth: We use Firebase Authentication (Google sign-in).
  • Payments: Subscriptions run through Stripe Checkout; card data never touches our servers.
  • Storage: Subscription status is stored under your UID in Firestore.
